The Middle period (c. 2050–1710 BCE)

The Middle Kingdom revived Egypt after a chaotic First Intermediate Period, featuring pharaohs like Mentuhotep II who reunified the land, expanded irrigation for agriculture, promoted literature and mining expeditions to Nubia and Sinai, and fostered a more balanced society, but it too fragmented amid invasions by the Hyksos.
